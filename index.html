About MelonJam:

MelonJam is a rather epic jam. However, its epicness is nothing in comparision to the Cupertino Game Development Club (GDC). Led by president Mr. Ryan Lee, GDC has made a name for itself throughout Cupertino High School. Mr. Ted McLeod, the club's advisor and an extremely well-respected Computer Science teacher, speaks highly of the GDC, placing it #3 in his greatest achievements of all time.
